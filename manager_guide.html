📊 Manager Dashboard Guide

This document explains how to monitor fleet health and safety compliance.

1. Risk Index (Ri)

The Ri is calculated based on cumulative biomechanical stress. Indices above 80% require immediate intervention or a mandatory rest period for the driver.

2. Intervention Protocol

When a "Critical" alert appears on the map, the manager should contact the unit to verify if mechanical assistance (forklifts/cranes) is required.
