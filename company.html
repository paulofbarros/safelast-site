Technical Note: Biomechanical Thresholds
The $R_i$ threshold defines the transition from manual handling to mechanical-only operations. According to SALA predictive models, an index above 85% indicates that musculoskeletal stabilizers (L5/S1 region) can no longer compensate for surface instability ($S_f$) and neuromuscular fatigue ($F_i$). Mandatory mechanical assistance is triggered to maintain compliance with Arbeidsmiljøloven § 4-4.
*This adjusts the Biomechanical Surface Factor ($S_f$) multiplier to account for extreme Norwegian weather conditions.
85%
Determines the point at which the Driver App triggers a "Mechanical Assistance Required" alert.
