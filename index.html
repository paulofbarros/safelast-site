Strategic Overview

SALA is a proprietary predictive framework designed to quantify and mitigate ergonomic risks in the Norwegian transport sector, focusing on reducing long-term sickness claims (sykemelding).

The Core Algorithm

BIOMECHANICAL RISK INDEX (Ri):

Ri = (Sf × Lw) + (Fi × Th)

The Science Behind SALA

Biomechanical Surface Factor (Sf): Operating on compressed snow or ice requires up to 80% more stabilizer muscle activation. SALA applies a 1.8x multiplier to account for increased torque on the lumbar region (L5/S1).

Neuromuscular Fatigue (Fi): Risk scales exponentially after 6h 30m of active duty. Vibration-induced fatigue leads to delayed motor response, increasing manual handling hazards.

Regulatory Compliance (Arbeidsmiljøloven § 4-4): SALA provides data-driven evidence for the Verneombud (Safety Delegate) to mandate mechanical assistance and ensure worker safety.
