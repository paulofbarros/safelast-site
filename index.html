Overview

SALA is a proprietary predictive framework designed to quantify and mitigate ergonomic risks in the Norwegian transport sector. We bridge the gap between traditional safety protocols and modern predictive data to reduce long-term sickness claims (sykemelding) and protect driver health.

The Core Algorithm

Ergonomic Risk Index Calculation:

Ri = (Sf × Lw) + (Fi × Th)
  • Sf (Surface Factor): Dynamic coefficients for sub-arctic conditions (Snow, Slush, Mud).
  • Lw (Load Weight): Impact of total tonnage handled.
  • Fi (Fatigue Index): Cumulative metabolic cost based on active duty.
  • Th (Time/Hours): Duration of continuous physical intervention.

Strategic Benefits

Transport Companies

Lower insurance premiums, reduced staff turnover, and full compliance with Arbeidsmiljøloven.

Drivers & Unions

Real-time "Go/No-Go" safety support to prevent Musculoskeletal Disorders (MSDs).

Regulatory Alignment

The SALA project is developed to complement the YSK (Statens vegvesen) professional competence standards, ensuring that data insights are practical, legal, and culturally relevant to the Norwegian workplace.
