Driver's Health & Safety Manual

🔬 Scientific Research Integration: This application logs anonymized biomechanical data to support ongoing PhD research in Occupational Health. Your participation helps establish new safety standards for logistics operations in Nordic climates.

The SALA (Safe-Load Analytics) system is your personal biomechanical assistant. By monitoring real-time physical strain, we prevent long-term injuries and medical leave through advanced data analysis.

🤖 SALA AI Update: The system now uses machine learning to predict muscle fatigue based on your individual movement patterns and the weather conditions in the corresponding region.

1. The Risk Index (Ri) & ML Engine

The Risk Index is a dynamic calculation powered by a Machine Learning engine that evaluates:

  • Cumulative Fatigue: Based on driving hours (initialized at 2.0h) and vibration exposure.
  • Dynamic Thermal Sync: Automatic adjustment of safety margins based on local temperature and tendon elasticity.
  • Mass Proportionality: The ratio between driver weight and handled payload.

2. Real-Time Alert Protocols

⚠️ YELLOW ALERT (50-79%): Elevated strain detected. Improve lifting posture and prioritize the use of mechanical aids.
🚫 RED ALERT (80%+): Critical musculoskeletal saturation. Cease manual handling immediately and perform a 5-minute active recovery break.

3. The Smart Scanner System

Every interaction with the [ ] SCAN PACKAGE button updates your risk profile:

  • Automated Logging: Scanned packages increment cargo weight by 0.01t for baseline risk calculations.
  • Manual Overrides: Use the "Heavy Packages" field for units exceeding 25kg to ensure accurate lumbar protection.

4. Environmental Factors

In temperatures below 0°C, the system triggers Vasoconstriction Compensation. This increases the safety buffer for muscle fibers and tendons, suggesting shorter intervals between manual tasks.

5. Data Privacy & Anonymization

SALA is a preventative tool. All research data is de-identified at the source. No personal identification is stored in the research database, ensuring your privacy while contributing to global occupational safety science.
