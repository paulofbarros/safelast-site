Technical Note: Baseline Fatigue

Why start with 2.0 hours?

Drivers are exposed to Whole-Body Vibration (WBV) during transit. According to ISO 2631-1, 2 hours of driving causes micro-swelling of spinal discs and reduced muscle stability.

SALA initializes with 2.0h as a Safety Margin to account for this accumulated metabolic fatigue before your first lift.
