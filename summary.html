SALA: Safe-Load Analytics

Executive Consolidation Summary (Norwegian Logistics Corridor)

1. Project Vision

SALA is an integrated Biomechanical Governance and advanced telemetry solution, specifically developed for the logistics sector in Larvik, Norway. The central goal is to mitigate spinal injuries (L5/S1) through real-time monitoring and algorithmic intervention.

Biomechanical Engineering

  • Ri (Risk Index): Core algorithm processing load, posture, and fatigue data.
  • L5/S1: Monitoring of compressive torque on lumbar discs.
  • Δf: Cumulative fatigue coefficient based on the 37.5h work week.

Regulatory Sovereignty

  • AML § 4-4: Total automation of compliance with the Norwegian Working Environment Act.
  • Arbeidstilsynet: Reports ready for government health and safety audits.
  • Verneombud: Dedicated interface for local safety delegates.

2. Digital Ecosystem Structure

The project is consolidated into the following technical modules:

  • Management Dashboard: Strategic fleet overview, real-time heatmaps, and YSK compliance tracking.
  • Master Glossary (Multilingual): Unified technical documentation in EN/NO for drivers and engineers.
  • Arctic Protocol: Dynamic safety adjustments for glare ice (CoF < 0.1) and extreme temperatures.
  • Mechanical Mandate: Automatic interruption protocol for manual handling when Ri > 85%.

3. Regional Context (Larvik Hub)

Deployment is centered on the Larvik Hub and the E18 corridor, integrating surface friction (Sf) data and specific Nordic conditions to calibrate alerts sent to drivers' mobile devices.
